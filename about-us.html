About Spice Garden

Our Story, Our Passion, Our Commitment

Our Vision

To be the premier destination for authentic Sri Lankan cuisine, preserving traditional flavors while creating memorable dining experiences that celebrate the rich culinary heritage of our island nation.

Our Mission

We are dedicated to serving exceptional Sri Lankan dishes prepared with the finest ingredients, traditional recipes, and genuine hospitality. Our mission is to share the warmth and flavors of Sri Lankan culture with every guest, whether dining in or taking away.

Our Location

Spice Garden is conveniently located in the heart of Negombo, Western Province, Sri Lanka. Our restaurant is easily accessible and provides a welcoming atmosphere where families and friends can gather to enjoy authentic Sri Lankan meals.

Address: 123 Beach Road, Negombo, Western Province, Sri Lanka

Opening Hours: Daily 11:00 AM - 10:00 PM

Service Types

Dine-In

Experience our full hospitality in our comfortable dining area. Enjoy your meal in a warm, traditional ambiance with attentive service. Perfect for family gatherings, business lunches, or romantic dinners.

Takeaway

Take the flavors of Spice Garden home with you. Our efficient takeaway service ensures your favorite dishes are prepared fresh and packed carefully for your convenience.

Our History

Spice Garden was founded in 2010 by the Perera family, whose passion for Sri Lankan cuisine spans three generations. What began as a small family-run eatery has grown into one of Negombo's most beloved restaurants. Our recipes have been handed down through generations, with each dish telling a story of Sri Lankan tradition and culture.

Over the years, we have remained committed to authenticity, using traditional cooking methods and sourcing spices from local farmers. Our success is built on the trust of our customers and our unwavering dedication to quality. Today, Spice Garden continues to serve the community with the same passion and authenticity that defined our humble beginnings.
